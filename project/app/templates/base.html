Unidade de Biologia Teórica e Computacional CBiot is a Center for Interdisciplinary Studies at UFRGS, where a large part of the activities of the Biotechnology Undergraduate Program and the Postgraduate Program in Cell and Molecular Biology are held. UBTEC is the unit where computational analyzes are performed to process large quantities of biological data, such as genomes, metagenomes and transcriptomes.
